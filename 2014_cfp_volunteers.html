Call for Student Volunteers

We are looking for dedicated and enthusiastic student volunteers to help "make it all happen." Volunteers commit to twenty hours of volunteer effort in exchange for conference registration. Non-local volunteers will also receive hotel lodging.

Serving as a student volunteer is a great way to get involved with the conference, meet lots of people, and help out in a big way.

To apply for a student volunteer position, please fill out this form. The deadline for applications is December 20, 2013. We will send out notifications by January 18, 2014.

For questions or more information, please contact the Student Volunteer Chairs:

  • Eric Ragan, Oak Ridge National Laboratory, USA
  • Gurjot Singh, Virginia Tech, USA

studentvolunteerschairs [at] ieeevr.org
