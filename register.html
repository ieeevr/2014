Registration

Registration is now open for IEEE VR 2014!

Early Bird registration deadline is February 28, 2014

Click here to register

Included within each registration category:

Full Week: USB Proceedings, All 3DUI and VR Sessions (Sat-Wed), Tutorials & Workshops (Sat-Sun), 3DUI Social Event (Sat night), Exhibitors Reception (Sun night), University Reception (Mon night), VR Banquet (Tue night)

VR Only: USB Proceedings, VR Sessions (Mon-Wed), Exhibitors Reception (Sun night), University Reception (Mon night), VR Banquet (Tue night)

3DUI, Tutorials, & Workshops Only: USB Proceedings, All 3DUI Sessions (Sat-Sun), Tutorials & Workshops (Sat-Sun), 3DUI Social Event (Sat night), Exhibitors Reception (Sun night)

One Day Conference Only: USB Proceedings, Access to Technical Sessions, Exhibits, and Breaks for One Day Only. If applicable, a ticket to the day’s social event must be purchased separately.

One Day Exhibits Only: Access to the Exhibits Venue, Research Demos, and Breaks for One Day Only. Does not include access to technical sessions. If applicable, a ticket to the day’s social event must be purchased separately. If multi-day exhibits access is desired, additional one day passes must be purchased onsite.

For registration questions, please contact vrreg@ieee.org.
